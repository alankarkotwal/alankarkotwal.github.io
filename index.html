Research interests

I work in computational imaging, where we combine illumination, sensing and computation in innovative ways to extract information from the environment in a way that a traditional camera cannot. I currently build optical imaging systems that use the wave nature of light to achieve exciting capabilities like seeing through scattering media and micron-scale depth acquisition, with applications in biomedical imaging and industrial fabrication. Some of these are possible only in the lab now: We're bringing them out in the open!

I look forward to specializing my research to real-life biomedical imaging situations, and exploring related fields like computational displays and holography in the future.
