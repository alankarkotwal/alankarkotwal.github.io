This is my primary field of interest. I like cosmology, stellar statistics and studying the large-scale structure of the universe.

What I've done

Image Photo-Z, Google Summer of Code 2014

A new pixel-level method for estimation of photometric redshifts (and hence distances) for distant galaxies using Machine Learning.

The IITB Mars Rover Project

Working towards autonomous navigation and vision-guided activities. Previously worked on on-board controls development and software.

Arkaroola Mars Robot Challenge 2014

Tested the Mars Rover prototype developed by the IITB Mars Rover Team in the south Australian outback. Participated in a series of exercises in Mars operations research conducted by Saber Astronautics which included simulated extra- vehicular activities in simulated space suits and explored Arkaroola geology to study its similarities to Martian geology and its usefulness as a Mars analogue site.

PCA-Lens Finder

A fast principal component analysis-based strong gravitational lens finder.

LC-3 RISC Processor on an FPGA.

Astro Stuff
